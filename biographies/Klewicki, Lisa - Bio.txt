Lisa Klewicki, Ph.D., is Dean of the Institute for the Psychological Sciences and Psy.D. Program Director at Divine Mercy University. She is a licensed clinical psychologist in the Commonwealth of Virginia.

Dr. Klewicki holds a Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology and a Master's degree in Theology, both from Fuller Theological Seminary in Pasadena, California.

She has been in clinical practice for over 21 years, conducting psychological assessments and psychotherapy for adults and couples. Dr. Klewicki has been a member of the IPS clinical faculty for 12 years, teaching basic clinical skills and psychological assessment to doctoral students.

Her research interests include psychological assessment of adolescents and adults — especially the use of assessment as a therapeutic technique — and the psychotherapy process, particularly the relationship between therapist and client as a tool for growth.
