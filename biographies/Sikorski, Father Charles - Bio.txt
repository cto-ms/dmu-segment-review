Rev. Charles Sikorsky, L.C., J.D., J.C.L., has served as President of Divine Mercy University (formerly the Institute for the Psychological Sciences) since 2007, guiding the institution's mission to integrate Catholic faith with the science of psychology. A member of the Legionaries of Christ, Fr. Sikorsky holds a Juris Doctor (J.D.) and a Licentiate in Canon Law (J.C.L.), bringing a distinctive legal and canonical perspective to his leadership of Catholic higher education and the formation of mental health professionals grounded in a Christian understanding of the human person.
