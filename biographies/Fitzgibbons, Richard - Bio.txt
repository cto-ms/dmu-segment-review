Richard Fitzgibbons, M.D., is the founder and director of the Institute for Marital Healing outside Philadelphia, where he has worked with hundreds of Catholic marriages and families over the past 40 years. He holds a B.S. from St. Joseph's University and an M.D. from Temple University School of Medicine, with psychiatric training at the Hospital of the University of Pennsylvania and the Philadelphia Child Guidance Center. He served as an adjunct professor at the Pontifical John Paul II Institute at the Catholic University of America and as a consultor to the Vatican's Congregation for the Clergy. He co-authored Forgiveness Therapy: An Empirical Guide for Resolving Anger and Restoring Hope.
